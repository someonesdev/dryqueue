📋 How to Use the Time Tracker

🕐 Logging Time

Timestamp Button: Click the "Timestamp" button to record the current time. Each click creates a new time entry in chronological order.

Automatic Pairing: Times are automatically organized in pairs (Start → End). The first click logs a start time, the second click logs an end time, and so on.

✏️ Editing Times

Edit Button (✎): Click the pencil icon in the top-right corner of any cell to edit that specific time entry.

Precision Editing: You can edit times down to the second (HH:MM:SS format) for accuracy.

Time Validation: The system ensures all times remain in chronological order. You cannot set a time earlier than the previous entry or later than the next entry.

🗑️ Deleting Entries

Delete Cell (✕): Click the red X next to the edit button to delete a specific time entry.

Deleted State: Deleted cells show red "00:00:00" text.

Auto Row Removal: If both cells in a row (start and end time) are deleted, the entire row will automatically disappear.

Restoration: Click the edit button on a deleted cell to restore it with a new time value.

📊 Calculating Totals

TOTAL Button: Shows a summary of pairs whose duration is at least 3 minutes. Durations under 3 minutes are omitted from the summary.

Display format: if the time is less than 60 seconds, it shows as seconds only; if it's 60 seconds or more, it shows as minutes and seconds.

Rounding: Rounding to minutes happens only on the final total (30s+ rounds up; <30s rounds down).

Requirements: You need at least 2 time entries (1 complete pair) and there must be no red 00:00:00 entries.

Automatic Copy: The summary is automatically copied to your clipboard.

💾 Data Persistence

Automatic Saving: All your time entries are automatically saved to your browser's local storage after every change.

Privacy-First: All data is stored locally on your device.

📸 Taking a Screenshot

Use your OS tools to take screenshots (Windows/Mac guides below).

🔄 Clearing Data

Clear: Clears all time entries and returns the tracker to its initial state.

🌙 Theme Options

Dark/Light Mode: Toggle at the bottom-right corner.
